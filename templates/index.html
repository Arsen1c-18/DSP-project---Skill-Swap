What is Association Rule Mining?

ARM finds "If A then B" patterns — the same technique e-commerce sites use for "Customers who bought X also bought Y."


Here, if many users who offer Skill A also offer Skill B, there's a natural clustering — these skills belong to the same domain or are typically learned together.


Support = % of users with both skills
Confidence = P(B | A) — probability of B given A
Lift = how much more likely than random (>1 = meaningful)
